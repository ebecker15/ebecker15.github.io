About Elyse Becker


Elyse Becker, founder of Not Alike Photography, is a 17 year old girl with a passion for photography.

She's loved photography from a very young age, and as she got older her passion for the art only grew.

She got her first real camera two years ago, and she's been taking pictures ever since.

She started taking clients for senior portraits last year and she was able to turn it into a profitable business; she puts the majority of her earnings towards her college savings.

She also has a strong love for Computer Science, especially web development, so she merged her two passions and built the Not Alike Photography website from the ground up using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

She loves soccer, hiking, music, photography (obviously), traveling & expereincing new cultures, robotics, computer science, and learning new things!
